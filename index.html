What It Does

Opportunity Discovery

Scans RSS feeds for contests, labs, and fellowships. Extracts deadlines, costs, and requirements automatically.

Your Agent evaluates the fit between your work and each opportunity. Shows detailed rationale for every score.

Package Generation

Creates professional query emails, multiple logline variants, and submission checklists tailored to each opportunity.

Pipeline Tracking

Track status from draft to submitted to follow-up. Set reminders. Never lose momentum.

Agent-Agent never sends emails for you. Ever. You review every package, edit as needed, and send manually. This is your momentum engine, not a spam bot.
